# Mapping of the ppap structure into ppap[1-7] for easy exploitation:

# first part
# <i have a> <pen> <i have an> <apple> <ppap_uuh> <apple pen#1>
#   -ppap1-          -ppap2-             -ppap3-

i have a; ppap1
i have an; ppap2
ppap_uuh; ppap3; replace

# second part
# <i have a 2> <pen> <i have> <pineapple> <ppap_uhh> <pineapple pen#1>
#   -ppap4-           -ppap5-              -ppap6-

i have a 2; ppap4
i have; ppap5
ppap_uhh; ppap6; replace

# third part
# <apple pen#2> <sweep> <pineapple pen#2> <sweep> <ppap_uuuh> <pen pineapple apple pen>
#                                                   -ppap7-

ppap_uuuh; ppap7; replace

# outside-of-ppap mapping of similar sounds for convenience

i have a 2; i have a; replace
pen pineapple apple pen; ppap
